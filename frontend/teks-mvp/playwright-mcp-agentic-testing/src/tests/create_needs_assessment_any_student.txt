Navigate to http://localhost:4200/students to view the students list.

STEP 1: Click on the FIRST "Open" button you find in the table (any student is fine for this test).

STEP 2: After navigation, click on the "Needs Assessments" or "Needs" navigation tab/link.

STEP 3: Click the "+ New Needs Assessment" button.

STEP 4: Fill in the Academic Needs textarea with: "Student demonstrates solid foundational reading skills."

STEP 5: Fill in the Support Services textarea with: "- Supplemental reading support 2x per week"

STEP 6: Fill in the Instructional Strategies textarea with: "- Repeated reading practice with feedback"

STEP 7: Fill in the Assessment Tools textarea with: "- WCPM tracking"

STEP 8: Click the "Create" button to submit the form.

SUCCESS INDICATOR: Look for URL parameter "?added=1" or success message showing the needs assessment was created.
